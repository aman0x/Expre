About Us

Expre – The Empiric Souvenir, is the unique podium to bond talent with an opportunity.

Expre offers to exhibit your talents globally. It’s an exceptional platform where you can register yourselves and get a chance to demonstrate the Art, Culture or hidden gift of capability & potential to the entire world with earnings. The world will come to you to experience your skills!

Many travellers on this globe are not just tourists, but have thrust to experience the real thrills. Expre helps to connect these travellers to the experience providers like you. And you being their host shall fulfil their outlooks by providing a pleasant hands on involvement of your skills with gain. In other words, Expre is the facilitator between the Expert and the Enthusiastic!

The Strength of Expre is, acting as a showcase for your proficiencies across the sphere.

Course of Expre’s Action

Expre talent hunters are continuously combing the market to bring various unexplored talents in front of the world.

This is how exactly Expre allows Experience providers to shine with us –

- Expre talent hunter team gathers profiles by means of various Social networks or an Experience provider can register himself / herself on www.expre.in.

- The actual screening of the profiles as per their experience levels is done by Expre talent hunter team. In screening, contacting Experience providers and exploring more about their capabilities is done.

- After finalising any of the talents, Expre team visits / inspects the actual site. - The Talent is approved as “Expre Approved!”, when he / she fulfils all the conditions as per Expre’s checklist. - Requirement gathering for an activity like duration, location, things to carry by travellers, costing etc. is done by the Expre team.

- A mutual contract is signed between Expre & the experience provider.

- All the talents & their providers are exhibited & promoted openly on www.expre.com.

- Travellers, registered on www.expre.com , buys the experience on the website. - The Experience provider is informed accordingly by Expre team for the bookings.

- Traveller is guided with all the required details by Expre team as directed by the experience provider & direct contact details of the experience provider. - Activity is conducted and completed as per the schedule.

- A happy traveller walks away with beautiful memories and leaves a feedback with Expre. - Payments of the Experience provider are done on fortnightly / monthly basis as per the contract payment terms.

Currently, Expre team is in the process of talent hunt enormously. Expre is the concept which allows a common man or local heroes to show and teach his / her talent to various people across the planet with good income options.
